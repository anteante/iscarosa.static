Practise

This could be a short introduction to the concept of Bodysex: Betty Dodson ran Bodysex workshops for over 25 years. They grew out of the consciousness raising groups feminists started in the 1970's. Women would gather together and talk first person about their personal lives. Betty took it one step further and had women answer two questions: how do you feel about your body & how do you feel about your orgasm. She called her workshops “physical and sexual consciousness raising” which she later shortened to “Bodysex”.

Virtual Bodsex
